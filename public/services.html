The Diamond Lux Program

Full Preservation.
Every surface corrected. Every surface sealed.

Every vehicle that comes through Diamond Lux receives the complete program — there is no partial version. We assess the paint with a thickness gauge, correct it to its maximum achievable clarity, seal every surface with professional-grade ceramic protection, and return the vehicle fully presented inside and out.

What varies between vehicles is not the scope — it is the depth of correction the paint requires. A vehicle with factory texture and light swirls demands a different approach than one with a fresh high-end respray or a finish prepared for show. The assessment determines the work. The work determines the investment.

When the panel requires it

Texture Leveling
— Wet Sanding

If the paint assessment reveals orange peel texture, factory surface irregularities, or overspray — and the paint thickness allows it — we level the lacquer at a microscopic scale before correction begins. This is the step that separates a polished finish from a true mirror. It is a multi-day, artisanal process that requires body shop-level expertise, a PTG reading on every panel, and a different calibration of investment entirely.

Not every vehicle needs it. Every vehicle that does, will know — because we will tell you at the assessment.
