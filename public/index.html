Our Philosophy

Most vehicles are cleaned. Few are truly restored.
Diamond Lux was founded on one belief: protection applied over imperfection is not protection at all.

A Standard Built
for Preservation

Every vehicle undergoes a structured restoration, refinement, and protection process — designed to improve appearance, preserve condition, and protect long-term value.

01

Assess

A comprehensive vehicle inspection under controlled lighting. Every defect documented before any work begins. No assumptions. No shortcuts.

02

Restore

Multi-stage paint correction removes swirls, scratches, and oxidation. The surface is refined to its true clarity before any protection is applied.

03

Protect

Premium ceramic coating applied to a properly corrected surface. Lasting protection that actually performs — because the foundation beneath it is sound.

04

Document

A written assessment delivered before and after every vehicle. Measurable results. Full transparency on what was achieved and why.

05

Maintain

An ongoing maintenance program designed to preserve the investment. Scheduled care that extends the life of every coating and treatment applied.
